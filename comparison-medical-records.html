The Clinical Standard.

Medical records represent the highest historical standard for documenting human life-critical decisions. AgDR v1.8 brings that same standard—mathematically hardened—to the world of autonomous agents.

Functional Analysis: EHR vs. AgDR

In healthcare, the Electronic Health Record (EHR) protects the patient and the physician through longitudinal audibility. AgDR applies these same principles to the digital "i" point of agentic thought.

Closing the Fiduciary Gap

The transition from human to autonomous decision-making often results in a "responsibility vacuum." AgDR closes this gap by ensuring that an AI's decision is never isolated. By recording the Reasoning Trace and the PPP Triplet at the moment of inference, the system establishes a record that carries the same weight—and the same duty—as a clinical entry in a patient's chart.
