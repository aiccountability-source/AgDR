Providence

The lineage of the intelligence. It tracks the provenance of the weights and data utilized in the inference, ensuring that the "beholder" can verify the integrity of the thought's origin.

Place

The jurisdictional and situational context. It locks the inference to a specific legal boundary (e.g., Ontario Statutory Duty), ensuring the action is authorized for its specific location.

"Where is this acting?"

Purpose

The teleological goal. Purpose is the subjective intent of the director translated into a machine-readable invariant. It prevents the model from diverging into unapproved objectives.
