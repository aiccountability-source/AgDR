Statutory Identity.

The PPP Triplet provides the metadata required to authenticate an electronic record under federal law.

A record without a defined intent and author is mere data. For an AI inference to carry legal weight, it must satisfy the requirements of Authentication and Authority. The PPP Protocol (Purpose, Place, Person) is the technical enforcement of these statutory pillars.

Purpose

Ensures the intent of the record is clear at the moment of creation. This satisfies the context required for a Business Judgment defense.

Place

Logs the specific compute node or hardware environment. Essential for proving System Integrity under CEA s.31.2.

Person

Identifies the Fiduciary Officer or authorized agent. This maps to the Signature requirements of the 1890 Act.

The Connection to Justice Canada

By defining these three points before an inference begins, we ensure the resulting AgDR is not a post-hoc guess. It is a contemporaneous record that meets the highest evidentiary standards in Canada.
